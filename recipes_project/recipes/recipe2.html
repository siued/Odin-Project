Chicken soup

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 celery
  • 1 leek
  • 1 parsnip
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 parsley
  • 1 thyme
  • 1 salt
  • 1 pepper

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in a pot
  2. Cover with water
  3. Bring to boil
  4. Simmer for 2 hours
  5. Remove chicken
  6. Remove meat from bones
  7. Return meat to soup
  8. Serve!
