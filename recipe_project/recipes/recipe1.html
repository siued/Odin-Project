Thai noodles

Ingredients

  • half a garlic
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Lime juice
  • 400g tofu
  • olive oil
  • rice noodles
  • peanuts

Instructions

  1. Fry tofu on oil in a separate pan until crispy
  2. Chop garlic
  3. Heat oil in a pan/wok
  4. Add garlic
  5. Fry until brown
  6. Add sugar
  7. Add soy sauce
  8. Add lime juice
  9. Add noodles and cover in water
  10. Bring to boil until noodles are done (read packaging)
  11. Make sure water is mostly evaporated by now
  12. When tofu is done, pour some soy sauce over it, then add it to noodles
  13. Add peanuts to taste
  14. Serve!
